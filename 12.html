Pakistan has the world,s largest deep-sea port in Gawadar, which lies southwest of the Arabian Sea along the coast line of Balochistan. As part of the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor and the Silk Route Initiative. Gawadar is of pivotal importance. The port city of the Gwadar is a hub of connectivity for the Corridor and is considered a lifeline in the region's economy.
