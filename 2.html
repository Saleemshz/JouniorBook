Katpana Cold Desert

The Worl'ds Highest cold desert

The world's highest cold desert, also known as Katpana Desert or Biama Nakpo, is a high-altitude desert located near Skardu in Pakistan.The desert cantains expanses of large sand dunes that are sometimes covered in snow during winter. Situated at an elevation of 7,303 ft above sea level, the deset stretches from Skardu to Shigar Valley.
