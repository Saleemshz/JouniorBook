Khewra Salt Mine

Pakistan's Baltoro Glacier in the Karakoram range is one of the world's longest valley glaciers. Though difficult to access, it is one of the most highlly trafficked region in the Pakistan because of mountaineering destitation like K2. Broad Peak, and the Gashernrum peaks at its head. The large portion of northern Pakistan population depends on melted water from the Karakoram glacier.
