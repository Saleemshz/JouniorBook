Khewra Salt Mine

Pakistan has the world's oldest and second largest salt mine in Khewrha, Punjab. Turning out an estimated 220 millions tons of salt over its lifetime. It is Pakistan largest mine.Covering an area of 110 square, km, with eleven separated story, the massisive mine has over 40km of tunnels running to a depth of approximattely 730 meters. The mine is famous for its pink Himalayan Salt.
